About SAKfabrics

Premium Pakistani fabric and clothing brand.

SAKfabrics - A Trusted Name

SAKfabrics is a Pakistani fabric and clothing store providing premium quality lawn, cotton, chiffon, silk, linen, khaddar, and bridal embroidery collections.

We serve customers across Pakistan with retail and wholesale fabric supply. Our mission is to provide luxury quality with affordable pricing and professional service.

Whether you need women stitched suits, men shalwar kameez, bridal wear, or winter shawls, SAKfabrics is your best choice.
